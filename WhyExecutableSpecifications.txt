Acceptance / Executable specificationd are used to test and document entire slices of the systems's functionalities

- Express in Given / When /Then express in natural language a particular test cases 
- Mimic user inputs and fine tune the initial context ( what will the shoe contain and what would be initial
hands for dealers and player and what cards will be dealt)
- The income being fixed, the expected outcome document and test the behaviour of the system.

- 7 scenarios have been written to cover most outcomes of the black jack game
- These scenarios along with unit tests can also drive developments
- The results of the excutable specifications are rendered in the an html report in .\Report of the project
